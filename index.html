About

Ph.D. candidate at Cleveland State University specializing in advanced manufacturing, powder metallurgy, and additive manufacturing of high-temperature alloys. Research focuses on microstructure-property relationships in Inconel 718 composites, spark plasma sintering, and high-temperature surface performance.

Research Areas

  • Additive Manufacturing (SLM, Binder Jetting, FDM, SLA)
  • Powder Metallurgy (Spark Plasma Sintering, HIP)
  • Surface Engineering & High-Temperature Oxidation
  • Tribology and Corrosion Performance
  • Microstructural Characterization (SEM/EDS, XRD, EBSD)
